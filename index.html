"Asenso Ibabao"

Barangay Ibabao, Cordova, Cebu is located in the northern-most part of the Municipality of Cordova. It is bound by Lapu-Lapu City in the north; Barangay Pilipog, Cordova in the west; Barangay Cogon and Gabi, Cordova in the East and Barangay Dapitan, Cordova in the south. The barangay is mostly residential, and considered as the biggest barangay in terms of land area and second to Barangay Poblacion in terms of population.

Crops used to grow in the barangay are cassava, apale, camote, ube and among others. Notably, the barangay is home of the renowned "PUTO BALANGHOY", a filipino delicacy, meticulously crafted from cassava root crop by traditional method of cooking.

According to the natives, the name "IBABAO" was coined from the word "KADABAO", a variety of cassava plant that is mostly grown in the area. The land feature of the barangay is mostly rocky with scattered soil surfaces favorable for small scale root crop farming. Other than that, The barangay name was also said to be devised due to the topographic feature of the land surface which is rocky, meaning, on the the top!

Since time immemorial, rope making, small scale root crop farming and fishing are the main livelihoods of the native Ibabaohanons, but as commercialization and indutrialization of the nearby cities of Cebu, Mandaue and Lapu-Lapu evolved, it commenced the opportunity for production jobs among Ibabaohanons.

These are some of the notable information of our ever dear barangay. Enjoy your stay and be; a proud Ibabaohanon! For more information, please feel free to visit the barangay executive building - hospitable and welcoming barangay staffs will be glad to assist you!
